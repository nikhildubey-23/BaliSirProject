What is Travel Insurance?

Travel insurance is a type of coverage that protects you from unexpected risks while traveling, whether it's a medical emergency abroad, a lost passport, delayed flights, or trip cancellations. It ensures that your finances, health, and belongings are taken care of while you're away from home, letting you focus on what really matters ‘enjoying your journey’.

Why is Travel Insurance Important?

Covers medical emergencies and hospital bills abroad

Compensates for flight delays, cancellations or missed connections

Includes COVID-19 related expenses and quarantines

Protects against lost or delayed baggage

Helps recover non-refundable costs of a canceled trip

Assists in case of lost passport or documents
