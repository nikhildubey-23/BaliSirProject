What is Fire & Burglary Insurance?

Life is full of surprises, Not all of them pleasant. Imagine waking up one day to find your shop, office, or home damaged by fire, or worse, looted. The emotional and financial blow can be overwhelming. That's where Fire & Burglary Insurance steps in — like a friend who's got your back when things go wrong.

Why Do You Need It?

Recover losses from damage caused by fire, lightning, explosions, etc.

Compensate for stolen goods in the event of a break-in or theft.

Cover repair or reconstruction costs for damaged property

Minimize business interruption and personal stress.

Coverage we offer

Fire Insurance

  • Riot, Strike, Malicious Damage
  • Natural calamities (like storms, floods, etc.)
  • Accidental damage due to fire or heat.
  • Fire accident
  • Lightning strike
  • Explosion/Implosion

Burglary Insurance

  • Theft by forceful entry
  • Loss or damage to contents due to break-in
  • Coverage for business stock, equipment, furniture, etc.
  • Optional: Coverage during transit or for cash stored securely
