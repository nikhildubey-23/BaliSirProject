Renewing Your General Insurance: Tips and Checklist

Renewing your policy on time ensures uninterrupted protection.

Renewal Checklist:

  • Check for No Claim Bonus (NCB)
  • Review and update sum insured
  • Compare premiums across insurers
  • Reconsider add-ons as per current needs
  • Ensure no break-in period (especially for motor)

Auto-renewal via credit card or online wallet helps avoid lapses!
