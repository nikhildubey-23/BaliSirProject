What is Workmen Compensation Insurance?

Workmen Compensation Insurance also known as Employee Compensation Insurance is designed to protect employers and support employees in case of work-related injuries, disabilities, or unfortunate loss of life during the course of employment.

Why it Matters?

Covers medical expenses for treatment of workplace injuries

Financial support for family in case of unfortunate death

Compensation for partial/total disability

Peace of mind for both employee and employer

Legal compliance for employers under the Workmen Compensation Act
