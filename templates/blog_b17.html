Tips to Lower Your Home Insurance Premium

Want comprehensive coverage at a better price? Here are smart ways to reduce your premium

Top Tips:

  • Install CCTV, fire alarms, and security systems
  • Opt for long-term policies (3 to 5 years) for discounts
  • Avoid over-insuring or under-insuring
  • Choose higher deductibles if you can afford it
  • Bundle with other policies (motor/travel) from the same insurer for multi-policy discounts

Tip: Always compare online before buying to get the best deal.
