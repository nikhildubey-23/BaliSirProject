Does Your Insurance Cover Natural Disasters?

Yes—but only if you’ve chosen the right plan.

Covered Under:

  • Home Insurance – Floods, earthquakes, storms
  • Car Insurance (Comprehensive only) – Damage from cyclones, floods, falling trees
  • Travel Insurance – Trip delays due to natural calamities

Not Covered If:

  • You only have third-party car insurance
  • Policy excludes “Acts of God”
  • Lapse in premium payments

Always confirm “Act of God” clauses and opt for disaster-specific add-ons in risk-prone areas.
