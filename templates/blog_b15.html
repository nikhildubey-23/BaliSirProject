Do You Really Need Travel Insurance? Here’s When It Matters Most

If you think travel insurance is just a “nice-to-have,” think again. Here's when it's absolutely essential

Scenarios Where Travel Insurance Helps :

  • Lost Baggage or passport abroad
  • Medical Emergency in a foreign country
  • Trip Cancellation due to illness or airline strike
  • Emergency Evacuation
  • Visa Requirement for countries like Schengen, USA, UAE

Whether you’re traveling for business, studies, or leisure, a small premium can save lakhs.
