Importance of Personal Accident Insurance for Working Professionals

Accidents don’t wait—and can derail your career and finances. Personal accident insurance gives lump sum compensation for such cases.

What It Covers:

  • Accidental death
  • Permanent total disability
  • Partial disability
  • Hospitalization due to accidents

Why Professionals Need It:

  • Ensures income protection during recovery
  • Affordable premiums
  • Complements your corporate health cover

Bonus: Many plans also offer education benefit for children in case of death/disability.
