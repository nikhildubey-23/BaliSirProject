Though both offer financial protection, health insurance and general insurance serve different needs.

Health Insurance Covers:

  • Hospitalization expenses
  • Surgeries & day-care procedures
  • Pre & post-hospitalization costs
  • Critical illness (with riders)
  • Maternity (if included)

General Insurance Covers:

  • Motor insurance (cars, bikes)
  • Home insurance
  • Travel insurance
  • Commercial property
  • Liability insurance
