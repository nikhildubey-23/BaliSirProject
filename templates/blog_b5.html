How to File a General Insurance Claim: Step-by-Step Guide

Filing a claim can be easy if you know the process.

Claim Filing Steps:

  • Inform the insurer ASAP via call/app.
  • File an FIR (for theft, accidents).
  • Collect evidence – photos, invoices, estimates.
  • Submit documents with the filled claim form.
  • Get damage surveyed (for vehicles or property).
  • Claim processed – through cashless or reimbursement.

Note: Keep policy number and emergency contacts handy at all times.
