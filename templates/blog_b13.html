Travel Insurance Claims: How to File Them Hassle-Free

Filing a claim doesn’t have to be a nightmare. Just follow these simple steps.

Step-by-Step Claim Process

  1. Inform the Insurer immediately via helpline or app.
  2. Collect Proof - like medical bills, police FIR, boarding passes.
  3. Fill the Claim Form – online or offline.
  4. Submit Required Documents – as per checklist.
  5. Wait for Verification – Surveyor may contact you.
  6. Receive Claim Amount – via direct bank transfer.

Note: Keep copies of all tickets, reports, and invoices while traveling.
