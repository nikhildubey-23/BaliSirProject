Travel Insurance for Students: What You Need to Know

If you're going abroad for studies, student travel insurance is a must—often mandated by universities.

Key Features of Student Travel Insurance

  • Medical coverage (including pre-existing conditions, depending on policy)
  • Loss of documents or laptop
  • Tuition fee reimbursement if you need to withdraw mid-semester
  • Baggage and personal liability coverage
  • Sponsor protection in case of sponsor’s untimely demise

Pro Tip: Check if your university accepts Indian insurance or requires local coverage. Always compare student-specific plans for best value.
