Home Insurance vs Home Loan Insurance, Know the Difference

While they sound similar, home insurance and home loan insurance serve different purposes

Feature Home Insurance Home Loan Insurance
Purpose Protects the property and contents Pays off the home loan if borrower dies
Coverage Fire, theft, natural calamities, etc. Outstanding loan amount
Beneficiary Homeowner Lender (Bank/NBFC)
Optional Yes Often bundled with home loan

Takeaway: Home insurance protects your property; home loan insurance protects your loan liability.
