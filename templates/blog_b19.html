What Does Home Insurance Typically Cover?

CA standard home insurance policy covers both the structure of your house and its contents. Here's a breakdown.

Coverage Includes:

  • Fire & Explosion
  • Natural Calamities like floods, earthquakes, storms
  • Burglary & Theft
  • Damage to Electrical Appliances
  • Structural Damage
  • Furniture, Electronics & Valuables (if opted)
