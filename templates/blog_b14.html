Top Things Your Travel Insurance Should Cover

Not all travel policies are created equal. Make sure your policy includes the following:

Must-Have Covers

  • ✅ Medical Expenses (including COVID-19)
  • ✅ Trip Cancellation or Curtailment
  • ✅ Loss of Passport or Luggage
  • ✅ Flight Delay or Missed Connection
  • ✅ Emergency Evacuation
  • ✅ Personal Liability

Pro Tip: If you're traveling with family or for an adventure trip, look for special plans with tailored coverage.
