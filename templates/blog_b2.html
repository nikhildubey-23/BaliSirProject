How to read & understand Policy Documents

Don’t skip the fine print! Here's how to break down your policy:

What to Check:

  • ✅ Policy Schedule – Insured name, IDV, premium
  • ✅ Inclusions/Exclusions
  • ✅ Coverage limits and sub-limits
  • ✅ Claim process and documentation
  • ✅ Add-ons and riders
  • ✅ Tenure and renewal clause

If unclear, ask your insurer or agent—never assume coverage.
