Insurance Types

Type Description Ideal for
Individual Health Insurance This is your personal health shield perfect if you’re looking for dedicated coverage just for yourself. Whether you’re young and healthy or managing a few health issues, an individual plan gives you the care you need, when you need it. You or one person
Family Floater Health Insurance One policy, one premium, one big safety net for your entire family—spouse, kids, even parents (in some cases). The sum insured is shared, so anyone can use it based on their need Your whole family
Senior Citizen Health Insurance As we grow older, health needs become more frequent and specialized. This plan is designed with that in mind—offering broader coverage for age-related illnesses, regular health checkups, and often, no-claim bonuses. Parents or anyone above 60+
Critical Illness Insurance Diseases like cancer, stroke, kidney failure, or heart attacks don’t just affect health—they can hit hard financially too. Critical illness insurance offers a lump sum payout upon diagnosis, so you can focus on recovery. Life-threatening illnesses
Maternity Health Insurance These plans cover delivery costs, newborn care, and even pre/post-natal expenses, so you can welcome your baby with joy, not financial stress. Expecting mothers
Personal Accident Cover This is your go-to plan for life’s most unpredictable moments. Whether it’s a minor injury or something more serious, personal accident insurance gives you financial support when you need it most. Accidental injuries / permanent disability
Top-Up & Super Top-Up Plans Already have a health plan, but want extra protection without spending too much? These are budget-friendly backups that kick in when your base policy limit is crossed. Ideal for unexpected, high medical bills. Boost your existing plan
